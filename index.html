01 — About

Educator by nature,
developer by craft

I teach, design, code, and occasionally make jokes on social media at Tinkercademy, a company I founded to help people — especially young people — learn to build with technology.

My background spans signal processing at Stanford, app development for Apple platforms, game design with Unity, and physical computing with micro:bit. I'm an Apple Certified Trainer, a Stanford Code in Place Section Leader, and a Minecraft Global Training Partner.

Whether it's a workshop on Swift, a book about micro:bit, or an app downloaded hundreds of thousands of times — I care about making things that are useful, learnable, and well-crafted.
